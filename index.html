college life

Introduction


Every person's life is greatly influenced by college. It sort of marks a beginning of a total new phase in our lives. It gives us the chance to discover new aspects of life, work together and form connections with others, and enhance both our social and technical skills.

From school life, college life is a significant adjustment. When we start college, we experience several changes. Your professors and buddies from your school days are no longer there to defend you as the transition to college is so abrupt. You will face several hurdles during your time in college. You will make friends in this new environment where there are many unexpected faces. And at the end of 4-5 years of the college, some of these friendship bonds become really strong.

College life teaches us how to interact with others and establish our own opinions. Students acquire their free will in college, which helps them grow more self-assured and collected. I'm sure each one of you too have had expectations regarding your college life. In many cases, the expectations do stand out true in reality as well.

Now, tighten up your seat belts... as we explore into the expectations and reality of my college(Hope your excited as well...so do enjoy along with me)!!
